\textbf{\LARGE Definitions and Theorems} \\
\large Background and Terms

\normalsize

\defn \textit{alphabet} $I$ of items: $I = \{a_1, a_2, \ldots , a_{|I|}\}$, where each $a \in I$ is an item.

\defn \textit{transaction} $t$: any non-empty subset $t$ of $I$ (i.e., $t \subseteq I$)

\defn \textit{dataset} $D$: a collection of transactions, where $D = \{t_1, t_2, \ldots , t_{|D|}\}$

\defn \textit{binary format} vs. \textit{sparse format}
